PROJECTS

Valley Lamps & Lighting service a wide range of clientele from customers undertaking renovations at home through to Architects and Engineers looking for lighting solutions that perform on multi-residential and Green Star office projects.

VLL in recent months have been the specification of choice by Architects and Engineers with our Australian made LED range of products and a number of European designer fittings.

Our business is largely about delivering a customer experience. At VLL We understand what is required by the market. We believe that our customers receive nothing but the very best when engaging VLL to supply solutions on their projects in terms of brief, communication, project management and delivery of our packaged solutions.
