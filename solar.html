In short, our systems perform in real world conditions and deliver on their promise of future cost avoidance because we carefully evaluate each case individually and only use the very best products manufactured by the world leaders in this technology.

What is the Government saying about Solar? Through its Renewable Energy Target (RET) Scheme, the Australian Government has pledged that by 2020, 20 per cent of Australia's electricity supply will come from renewable sources. This Scheme provides those who invest in Solar Systems with the confidence that their investment is not only green and socially responsible, but will be guaranteed by the government for the duration of the program.

Government benefits - In addition to generous State guaranteed feed-in-tariffs which provide ongoing returns for electricity exported to the grid, home and business owners can also benefit from solar credits (STCs) which subsidise the installation cost.

I don't understand, what's an STC?

Don't worry if you do not understand how the Government rebate structure works, at VLL we are here to make things easy! Typically our contractor will ask you whether you would like to keep your certificates to trade in at a later date however most clients choose to sign these over to the installing contractor in order to receive an instant discount on the cost of installing your new PV system.
