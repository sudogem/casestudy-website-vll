PRODUCTS

Valley Lamps & Lighting are renowned for bringing the best in European design to Australia.

Most of our work is project orientated therefore please allow up to eight weeks for delivery of your goods out of Europe however, we display and stock a selected amount of these pieces at our showroom in Fortitude Valley if you cannot wait.

Please contact us for specification sheets and product information should you wish to use some of our pieces on your next project.

The Elegant Range

Our own selection of beautifully hand crafted Australian made LED products ranging from downlights through to pendants. The Elegant range consists of products for most applications and utilizes the latest Japanese chip technology. All fittings are hand soldered and quality controlled in Sydney to eliminate any faults associated with importing finished product from overseas. We manufacture our fittings for some of the most respected names in the Corporate retail industry and provide extensive warranties along with after sales support.
