SUSTAINABILITY

VLL provide total project management solutions for contractors when faced with a daunting Design and Construct project. We have teamed up with three of the most respected names in the industry, as a result we are pleased to offer the following services -

  • BCA Energy Efficiency Compliance
  • NABERS Ratings & Certification
  • Energy Audits
  • Green Star submission
  • General ESD initiatives
  • Electrical Engineering (Design to implementation)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Design to implementation)
  • Fire Protection Services
  • Maintenance & Asset management

Our Engineers have hundreds of years collective experience of all projects and industries including Office fit-out, Aged Care, Airports & Aviation, Healthcare, Defence, Hotels and clubs, Laboratories, Local, State and Federal Government, Retail, Schools and sports facilities.

VLL Projects work in conjunction with some of the most renowned industry professionals on a number of sustainability and ESD initiatives. We train our people in the awareness of Green Star and have our own NABERS accredited assessor in training to perform energy audits through our consultancy.

VLL is committed to being involved in the Green Building Movement and can provide expert advice with solutions on a number of projects.

Please use the contact us information on this website or alternatively give us a call to find out how VLL can provide cost effective design solutions from small to large scale projects.
