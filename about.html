About Us

Valley Lamps & Lighting has been working to create lighting solutions through innovation and consultation with designers, engineers and architects to transform your perfect space. We deliver our solutions to the market with a unique approach by paying attention to detail, listening to our clients needs and implementing awesome designs in order for our clients to create freedom of expression through light.

What we do?

VLL provide tailored solutions to our clients from concept creation through to design and certification of projects in accordance with BCA J6 requirements.

We provide ESD initiatives and Engineering Services from GBCA Green Star design and submission through to National Australian Built Environmental Rating Systems (NABERS). In summary we can provide everything your client would need in relation to building services and energy efficiency.

We have a specialist project lighting division that provide solutions for major developments and Corporate Retail clients. All of our project clients benefit from a full BCA J6 Compliant design and certification including 3D rendering images and a dedicated project manager to deliver the task at hand.
